## 8. AUDIENCE OVERLAYS

Overlays adjust density, evidence register, archetype mix, and pacing — never the token system's structure or the story. Summary table, then specifics:

| Dial | Executive | Presales/technical | Marketing/external |
|---|---|---|---|
| Words/slide (presented) | ≤30 | ≤40 (may +1 density step) | ≤25; dividers ≤15 |
| Archetype weight ↑ | Statement, Evidence, Comparison | Architecture-diagram, Showcase, Comparison | Full-image, Statement, Journey |
| Archetype weight ↓ | Architecture detail, Showcase minutiae | Full-image mood | Dense Evidence, tables |
| Evidence register | pre-computed deltas, one number/moment | real artifacts, protocols, honest benchmarks | one proof anchor per promise |
| Diagram depth | name+type only, no protocols | full connector labels + tech | avoid; schematic only |
| Appendix | mandatory, heavy | mandatory (configs, full tables) | light |
| Pacing | fast, titles carry all | measured, demo-anchored | tight, emotional peaks engineered |

### 8.1 Executive
- **Titles-only must carry the whole argument**: read the assertion headlines in sequence as a standalone test — execs consume the layer-cake stripe and sample beneath [NNg §2, AE-A1].
- One number per moment; hero-stat treatment for the decision-driving figure; comparisons pre-computed (deltas, gaps vs goal — "do the math for the viewer") [Duarte §11, Zen #20].
- Diagrams compressed to name+type, protocols dropped [Ev §2.2]; detail exiled to an appendix with information scent ("full model in Appendix B"), ≤2 disclosure levels [NNg §5].
- Emphasis must look native and calm, not promotional — skeptical exec audiences discount decorated callouts (banner-blindness analogue) [NNg §3].
- Exec send-aheads are slidedocs: 100–200 words/page (hard ceiling 250), rigid 5-level text hierarchy, 50–70-char lines, real column grid, emphasis by weight/case never color [Duarte §4/§10 (MED-HIGH)].
- Cut "me-ness": no credentials openers or About-us fronts; first content slide addresses the audience's situation [Duarte §1].
- End on ONE verb-first action, accent-dominant; secondary options visibly subordinate [PC §5.3].

### 8.2 Presales / technical evaluation
- Evidence register: real screenshots, real data, architecture diagrams — proof over polish [PC §6.1]. Protocols and technology labels stay on connectors [Ev §2.3]; acronyms defined.
- Progressive diagram builds in live demos; grayed-full-structure variant when the audience needs the whole shape ("here's where it fits in YOUR stack") [Ev §2.5].
- Comparison archetype earns heavier use (ours/theirs, before/after) — with identical-framing discipline [Ev §1.6].
- Density may rise one step (body 18–20, more nodes) but the Glance Test still binds anything projected; deep tables/configs → appendix, referenced with scent.
- Honesty rules are non-negotiable here: truncated axes or cherry-picked deltas destroy technical credibility on contact [Tufte-B3, Ev §3.5].

### 8.3 Marketing / external
- Emotional register rises: Full-image and Statement archetypes carry more of the deck; picture-superiority is strongest for concrete, common subjects with delayed recall — exactly this condition [Zen #6].
- Conceptual/art-directed photography only with a genuine art-direction budget and a unifying treatment — never stock [PC §7 conflict-2, Tells-D1].
- Brand voice may take the radius/weight/palette temperament dials to expressive settings — still within one coherent micro-aesthetic per deck [Tools-B11].
- Claims still need evidence anchors: one proof point per promise (stat, screenshot, named result); decoration cannot substitute [Tufte-B9].
- Pacing tightens: more Statement/Minimal beats, ≤15 words on divider-class slides, S.T.A.R. moment mandatory [Duarte §12, PC §2.2].
